Key features

Everything you need to recover a device

One dashboard, every registered phone — from live location to a remote wipe, if it comes to that.

◎

Live GPS tracking

Real-time location, movement history, and last-known position on an interactive map.

⛁

Battery & connection status

See battery percentage, charging state, and whether the device is on Wi-Fi or mobile data.

◈

SIM-change alerts

Get notified the moment a SIM card is swapped — a key early signal of theft in Kenya.

♫

Ring on silent

Trigger a loud ring remotely, even if the phone is set to silent or vibrate.

▣

Lock screen message

Display a custom message and callback number directly on the lock screen.

⌗

Remote lock & wipe

Lock the device instantly, or erase data as a last resort to protect your information.

✎

Lost device reports

File a report, notify emergency contacts, and get email or SMS alerts on activity.

▤

Multi-device support

Register and monitor several Android devices — for family or company fleets.

⚿

End-to-end encryption

Every device report and command travels encrypted; only the verified owner can see it.
